How to Play

GOAL

Eat as many dots as possible in 90 seconds. Each dot scores 10 points. Don't hit the walls or your own tail.

CONTROLS

Arrow keys or WASD to steer. On mobile, swipe in any direction. The game starts on your first move.

SPEED

Every 5 food eaten, the snake speeds up. Manage your corners carefully at high speed.

CHALLENGE MODE

Food spawns are seeded — same code means the same food positions for everyone. Share your code for a fair async head-to-head.
